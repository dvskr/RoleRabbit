# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

## 1. Introduction

RoleRabbit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our portfolio creation platform.

This policy complies with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) requirements.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password (hashed)
- **Profile Information**: Bio, avatar, social media links
- **Portfolio Content**: Text, images, projects, work experience
- **Payment Information**: Billing details (processed by Stripe)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Pages viewed, features used, time spent
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address (anonymized)
- **Analytics**: Portfolio views, shares, exports (anonymized)
- **Cookies**: See our Cookie Policy below

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **OAuth Providers**: When you sign in with Google, GitHub, etc.
- **Payment Processors**: Transaction details from Stripe

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Provide Services**: Create, host, and publish your portfolios
- **Improve Platform**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, add features
- **Communicate**: Send service updates, newsletters (with consent)
- **Security**: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, enforce terms
- **Legal Compliance**: Comply with laws and regulations
- **Analytics**: Track portfolio performance (anonymized)

## 4. Data Protection

### 4.1 Security Measures

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Control**: Role-based access control (RBAC) with strict permissions
- **Authentication**: Secure password hashing (bcrypt), MFA support
- **Audit Logging**: All sensitive operations logged with timestamps and IP addresses
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and penetration testing

### 4.2 Data Storage

- **Location**: Data stored in secure data centers (US/EU)
- **Backup**: Daily encrypted backups with 30-day retention
- **Isolation**: Your data is isolated and never shared with other users

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

### 5.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all your personal data at any time through your account settings or by contacting us.

### 5.2 Right to Rectification

You can update or correct your information through your account settings.

### 5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data. We provide a 30-day grace period before permanent deletion.

### 5.4 Right to Data Portability

You can export all your data in JSON format through your account settings.

### 5.5 Right to Restrict Processing

You can opt-out of analytics tracking and marketing communications.

### 5.6 Right to Object

You can object to processing of your personal data for marketing purposes.

### 5.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Active Accounts

- **Account Data**: Retained while account is active
- **Portfolio Data**: Retained while portfolio exists
- **Analytics**: Anonymized data retained for 12 months

### 6.2 Deleted Accounts

- **Grace Period**: 30 days before permanent deletion
- **Audit Logs**: Retained for 7 years for compliance (user ID only, no PII)
- **Backups**: Removed from backups within 90 days

### 6.3 Inactive Accounts

- Accounts inactive for 3+ years may be anonymized or deleted

## 7. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We share data only in these cases:

### 7.1 Service Providers

- **Hosting**: Vercel, Supabase (infrastructure)
- **Email**: SendGrid (transactional emails)
- **Payment**: Stripe (payment processing)
- **Analytics**: Plausible (privacy-focused, anonymized)

All service providers are GDPR-compliant and bound by data processing agreements.

### 7.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to:
- Protect rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or security threats
- Comply with legal obligations

### 7.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred to the new owner with notice to you.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

### 8.1 Cookie Types

- **Necessary Cookies**: Essential for platform functionality (cannot be disabled)

### 8.2 Cookie Control

You can manage cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner. You can change preferences anytime in your account settings.

### 8.3 Do Not Track

We honor Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website.
